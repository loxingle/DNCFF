Yellow Roses.

1. Plant yellow roses where they receive full sun with light shade in late afternoon where summers are hot.
2. Choose a spot away from large trees and other shrubs so that the rose bush isn't forced to compete for water and nutrients.
3. Before planting the rose, dig a hole as deep as the plant's root ball and fill it with water.
4. Keep the soil moist but not saturated after planting.
